# Stakeholder Analysis

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|------------|------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| IT Service Manager | Oversees IT operations | Reduce SLA breaches | Lack of visibility into ticket risk | 30% reduction in SLA violations |
| Field Service Engineer | Resolves incidents | Efficient task allocation | Overloaded or uneven workload | Balanced workload distribution |
| Service Desk Agent | Logs and monitors tickets | Fast ticket handling | Manual prioritization | 20% faster ticket processing |
| IT Administrator | Maintains system | System uptime and reliability | System crashes or downtime | 99.9% uptime |
| Business Stakeholder | Depends on IT services | Minimal downtime | Delayed issue resolution | Improved system availability |
| End User (Employee) | Reports issues | Quick issue resolution | Long waiting times | Faster response times |
